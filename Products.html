Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our security products

Our AI-driven security solutions use machine learning algorithms to analyze patterns and behaviors across your network, applications, and systems. By establishing baselines of normal activity, our technology can quickly identify anomalies that may indicate security threats. The system continuously learns and improves its detection capabilities, adapting to new threats and reducing false positives over time.

Yes, our security products are designed to integrate seamlessly with your existing security infrastructure. We support integration with popular SIEM solutions, identity providers, cloud platforms, and other security tools through standard APIs and connectors. Our professional services team can assist with custom integrations for specialized or legacy systems to ensure a comprehensive security posture.

Our products support a wide range of compliance standards including GDPR, HIPAA, PCI DSS, SOC 2, ISO 27001, NIST Cybersecurity Framework, and more. Our compliance tools automate evidence collection, control mapping, and reporting to streamline the compliance process. Additionally, our solutions themselves are built with security and compliance in mind, helping you maintain a strong security posture while meeting regulatory requirements.

Deployment timelines vary based on the products selected and the complexity of your environment. Cloud-based solutions can typically be deployed within days, while more comprehensive enterprise deployments may take 2-4 weeks. Our implementation team works closely with your IT and security teams to ensure a smooth deployment process, including configuration, integration, and knowledge transfer. We also offer expedited deployment options for organizations with urgent security needs.

We provide tiered support options based on your plan. All customers receive access to our knowledge base, documentation, and community forums. Standard support includes email and ticket-based assistance during business hours. Professional plans include priority support with faster response times and extended hours. Enterprise customers receive 24/7 dedicated support with a named account manager and regular security reviews. We also offer optional managed services for organizations that prefer to outsource security operations.
